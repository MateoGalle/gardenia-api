# Gardenia Product Vision

## Product Overview

Gardenia is an e-commerce platform for a small women’s accessories business currently selling through third-party marketplaces such as Facebook Marketplace and Amazon Handmade.

The goal of the platform is to provide Gardenia with its own secure, branded, and customer-friendly online storefront.

## Problem Statement

Gardenia currently depends on external marketplaces, which creates challenges related to fragmented sales channels, customer interactions, payments, and exposure to intermediaries or scams.

## Target Users

- Customers in Canada looking for women’s accessories
- Quality-conscious buyers interested in unique handmade or artisanal products
- Gardenia administrators managing products, inventory, orders, and customer engagement

## Product Goal

Build a secure and user-friendly e-commerce platform that allows customers to browse, search, save, and purchase Gardenia products directly from the brand’s own website.

## MVP Scope

The MVP should include:

- Dynamic product catalog
- New arrivals section
- Clearance section
- Product detail pages
- Product images
- Product filtering
- Basic search
- Shopping cart
- Secure checkout foundation
- Bilingual support foundation
- Wishlist foundation
- Brand and legal trust information

## Future Enhancements

- Virtual assistant
- Advanced customer accounts
- Social media integrations
- Advanced reviews and recommendations
- Analytics and SEO improvements
